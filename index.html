Frequently asked questions

Braggy is all about having fun while achieving your goals. We believe that betting on your goals is a great way to stay motivated and to have fun with your friends. We also believe that betting on your friends’ goals is a great way to support them and to have fun with them.

Once you set a goal, your friends can put their money on your triumph or your downfall. If you achieve your goal, you collect the cash from those who bet against you. If you fail, those who bet on your failure take the winnings.

We believe a higher pot does not increase the fun at some point. Therefore, we have set a maximum bet of $100. There is no minimum bet.

You can ask your friends for the terms to cancel your bet. If you have a good reason, they will probably agree to it. If you don’t, you will have to pay up.

We will take care of that. Once you have won a bet we will send the money to your bank account. We will also send you (and all of your friends) a notification to let you know that you have won.

You can support your friends by betting on thier goals. In the end it does not matter if you bet on them or against them. Both will be some extra motivation.

Your cash is safe as houses. We use top-notch security systems to protect your transactions. This is all about the thrill of the chase and the sweet smell of success!
